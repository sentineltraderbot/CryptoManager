Sentinel Trader Bot Whitepaper

Introduction

Sentinel Trader Bot is a next-generation automated trading platform that leverages artificial intelligence and blockchain technology to provide users with a seamless, decentralized trading experience. By integrating AI-driven strategies with smart contract automation, Sentinel Trader Bot empowers users to maximize their trading potential while maintaining full control over their assets.

Vision

Our vision is to create an ecosystem where traders can automate their strategies using cutting-edge AI while benefiting from the transparency and security of blockchain technology.Sentinel Trader Bot aims to revolutionize the trading landscape by offering:

  • AI-powered trade execution
  • Blockchain-backed transparency and security
  • Decentralized ownership and governance

Token Utility ($SENTBOT)

The $SENTBOT token is the native utility token of the Sentinel Trader Bot platform. It serves multiple functions, including:

  • Airdrop Participation: Users can claim $SENTBOT tokens during the early distribution phase.
  • Trading Fees & Discounts: Use $SENTBOT to pay for platform fees and receive discounts.
  • Staking & Rewards: Stake $SENTBOT to earn additional rewards and access premium trading strategies.
  • Governance: Token holders can vote on platform upgrades and policy changes.

Fixed Supply

The total supply of $SENTBOT is capped at 100 million tokens, ensuring scarcity and long-term value.

Smart Contract Mechanism

To ensure a seamless experience, Sentinel Trader Bot utilizes smart contracts on the Solana blockchain. The key functionalities include:

  • Token Purchase via Smart Contract: Users can send SOL to a smart contract and receive $SENTBOT in return.
  • Future Integration: Smart contracts will be expanded for additional trading features.
